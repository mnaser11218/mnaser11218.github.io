# SecretBee Support

Welcome to the official support page for **SecretBee** 🐝

SecretBee is a fun and engaging spelling bee game designed for both kids and adults. Whether you're practicing on your own or competing in remote spelling bee challenges, we're here to help you have the best experience possible.
